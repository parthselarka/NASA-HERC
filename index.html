NASA HERC

Team Mushak

Team Mushak, a group of ambitious 18-year-old first and second-year students from India, is representing the nation at NASA's Human Exploration Rover Challenge (HERC) in the Remote-Controlled section. As the only Indian team selected, we're proud to showcase India's emerging talent in space innovation. Tasked with designing a rover to navigate simulated extraterrestrial terrains, we aim to prove that India's next generation of engineers is ready to excel on the global stage and contribute to the country's leadership in technology and space exploration.
